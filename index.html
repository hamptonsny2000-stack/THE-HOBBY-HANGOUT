What is Whop?

We use Whop to securely manage memberships, process subscription payments, and automatically grant access to our Discord community. It keeps billing, renewals, and access in one centralized platform.

Membership & Billing
Cancel anytime
You can cancel your membership at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees and no long-term contracts. Your access will remain active through the end of your current billing period.
No commitment
The 7-day free trial requires no long-term commitment. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged. Membership renews automatically after the trial unless canceled.
Refund policy
Because membership grants immediate access to the private marketplace and community, we do not offer refunds once a billing cycle has begun. If you experience a billing error or technical issue, contact support within 7 days and we will review it promptly.

Help

FAQ

Quick answers for signup, payments, and safe transactions.

What if I have a Discord or Whop issue?
We’re not Discord or Whop support. If you hit a technical issue, we’re happy to suggest fixes, but you may need to contact Discord/Whop support for account-level problems.
Can I cancel during the free trial?
Yes. Cancel before the trial ends and you will not be charged. Membership renews monthly unless canceled.
Why do you use Whop?
Whop manages memberships, billing, renewals, and automatically unlocks Discord access. It keeps everything secure and centralized.
How do I get access after purchase?
After checkout, Whop will prompt you to connect Discord. Once connected, access is granted automatically.
